Our Partners

Good working relationships among various stakeholders are crucial to the successful performance of the Nyeri Chai Farmers Association (NCFA). From information sharing to regular consultations, these relationships must be developed, managed, and sustained.

Stakeholder Engagement

NCFA has involved various stakeholders in its operations at different levels of engagement. **Traidcraft/Transform Trade** has been the only donor so far, providing material support to the Association.

In the recently concluded project, NCFA partnered with the **Federation of Kenya Agricultural Farmers (KENAFF)** for implementation. For its lobbying and advocacy work, the Association has initiated engagements with various stakeholders who are either currently supporting or have the potential to support the Association.

Stakeholder Categories

Within Nyeri County:

  • The Host County Government
  • Office of the County Women Representative
  • Offices of Members of Parliament in the five catchment areas
  • Office of the Senator for Nyeri County

Outside of Nyeri County:

  • Office of the President
  • Relevant Government Ministries, particularly Agriculture and Trade
  • KTDA and its subsidiary companies
  • Institutions such as KTGA, EATTA, and TRFK

NCFA will continue to grow these relationships and develop new partnerships for the benefit of our farmers. Together, we can create a sustainable and empowered community of tea farmers in Nyeri County.
