Members Benefits

  • Strong bargaining power.
  • Avenue for lobbying and advocacy.
  • Training on smallholder farmers' rights, gender issues, and new farming technologies.

How to Join Nyeri Chai Farmers Association

Requirements:

  • Must have belonged to one of the five factories in Nyeri County.
  • Must have registered in the association with 100 Ksh as a membership fee, renewable yearly (subject to change).
  • Must have a personal Grower No in one of the tea collection centres within the five factories.
  • Must be ready to follow the terms and conditions in the Nyeri Chai Farmers Association Constitution.
